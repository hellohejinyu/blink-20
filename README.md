## What is the 20-20-20 Rule?

The [American Optometric Association](https://www.aoa.org/) recommends:
> **Every 20 minutes**, take a **20-second break** and focus your eyes on something **20 feet (6 meters) away**.

This simple habit relaxes the focusing muscles inside the eye, reducing fatigue and preventing long-term damage.
